Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2024

1. Information About Us

The Bee6 Bot is a Discord bot operated by an independent development team. Our privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

2. What Data We Collect

We only collect the minimally required data for bot functionality:

  • Discord user ID (for identification)
  • Discord server ID (for server-specific settings)
  • Bot usage statistics (command calls)
  • Server configuration settings

3. How We Use Your Data

The collected data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Provision of bot functions
  • Server moderation and management
  • Improvement of bot performance
  • Bug fixing and support

4. Data Storage

All data is securely stored on our servers and protected with modern encryption methods. We only store data as long as it is necessary for the provision of our services.

5. Data Sharing

We never sell or rent your data to third parties. Data is only shared under the following conditions:

  • Legal obligations
  • Discord policy compliance
  • Protection from fraud or abuse

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored data
  • Correction of incorrect data
  • Deletion of your data (right to be forgotten)
  • Data portability

7. Cookies and Tracking

The Bee6 Bot uses no cookies or tracking technologies. All data is processed directly through the Discord API.

8. Security

We implement modern security measures to protect your data, including encryption, regular security audits, and access controls.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. All changes will be published on this page.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact our data protection officer through our Discord server or via email at privacy@bee6bot.de
