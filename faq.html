Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Bee6

How do I add Bee6 to my Discord server?

+

Simply click the "Add to Discord" button on our homepage, select your server, and grant the necessary permissions. Bee6 will join your server within seconds!

Is Bee6 free to use?

+

Yes! Bee6 offers a comprehensive free tier with all essential features. We also have premium plans for advanced features like priority support and enhanced performance.

What permissions does Bee6 need?

+

Bee6 requires standard permissions including Send Messages, Embed Links, Manage Messages, and Connect to Voice Channels. The exact permissions depend on which features you want to use.

How do I configure Bee6 for my server?

+

Use the /settings command to access Bee6's configuration panel. From there you can customize moderation levels, music settings, and much more!

Can I use Bee6 for music?

+

Absolutely! Bee6 supports high-quality music streaming from YouTube, Spotify, and SoundCloud. Use /play to start playing music in your voice channel.

What happens if Bee6 goes offline?

+

We maintain a 99.9% uptime! In the rare case of downtime, we have backup systems in place. Check our status page or join our support server for real-time updates.

How do I get help with Bee6?

+

For support, join our official Discord server where our team is ready to help! You can also check out our documentation or use the /help command.

Can I suggest new features?

+

We love hearing from our community! Feature suggestions can be submitted through our Discord server. Popular requests often make it into future updates.

Is my data safe with Bee6?

+

Yes! We only store essential data for bot functionality and never share it with third parties. Read our Privacy Policy for detailed information.

How do I update Bee6's permissions?

+

To update permissions, remove Bee6 from your server and re-invite it with the new permissions. Your settings will be preserved!
